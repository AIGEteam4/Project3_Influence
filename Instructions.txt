HOW TO USE:
Scroll with your mouse or press the Q and E keys to zoom in and out.
Left mouse click and drag anywhere to pan the camera around.
You may also press the WASD keys or the arrow keys to pan the camera.

Toggle whether you will spawn a unit on the red team or green team by pressing the "Team" button in the upper left corner.
Use the drop-down menu in the upper left corner to select what type of unit you would like to spawn.
Right mouse click on the terrain to place a unit at the point where you clicked.

The terrain's influence map is represented as a grid in the upper right corner of the screen. It will automatically update each time
a new unit is spawned.
